School Activities

School Library

Established: 2010

Our school library is full of amazing books for children to read. There are comfy bean bags for students to relax on, and a cash register station to check out your book. Loaded with over 100+ amazing books, of all kinds of genres and ages.



School Auditorium

Established: 2013

Our school auditorium is often used for multiple things. We use it for plays, shows, guest speakers, picture day and more. Other times we use it as a lunch room. Located in the multiuse room, we love to see our wonderful students' talent here! On the back, there is a large area with yummy food to eat. We also have rainbow decorations for the background, too!

School Events

Student Council
The Heart of Zanker

The Student Council always work hard to prepare school events for Zanker Scholars. They always contribute to the school and sets up events such as Girl Start, Chorus, and the Talent Show. "We love seeing our Zanker Jaguars come together!" -Student Council

What's New

  • New School Lunch

    The food Zanker serves has a variety of protein and has quick servings. The staff members porvide additional/special food support for those who have specific allergies. A student can set an account for themselves along with a handy lunch card with their Student ID and name. It helps the Nutrition Staff to keep the school canteen going. Students can carry lunch either with a tray that consists with a mini box of creamy milk, a side dish with organic fresh fruits and veggies such as carrots, pears, apples, or lettuce, and two options of the main dish. Students can also carry lunch from home. Hot Lunch is very cheap but has yummy food enough to make you energetuc. A quote says "We don't care about the cash we get; we care that our customers love and want our food." And as always, diversity is the key.
